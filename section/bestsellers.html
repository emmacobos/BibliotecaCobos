Best Sellers

Publication date

September 14, 2008

Plot

The story is set in Panem. There are twelve poor districts governed by the rich Capitol. The Capitol organises the Hunger Games every year to punish the districts. One boy and one girl fight in a battle. Only one person will live. The story is about Katniss Everdeen. Her younger sister, Prim, is chosen to be a “tribute”, but Katniss volunteers to go instead. The other “tribute” from District 12 is a boy named Peeta.
